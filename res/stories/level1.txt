You awaken in a labyrinth of shifting corridors; your memories erased. Cold walls hum with an unnatural glow, and distant whispers taunt your confusion.
A disembodied voice echoes: “Escape requires truth.” But truth is fractured here.
Time flickers—clocks run backward, shadows defy logic. Are you dreaming? Trapped? Or is this a fabricated reality?
